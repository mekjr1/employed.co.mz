Employed — logo concepts

Five directions for a refreshed Employed brand mark. Each is rendered as an icon at 96 / 48 / 24 / 16 px, plus a horizontal lockup, on both light (#F9FAFB) and dark (#27272B) surfaces.

Palette is anchored on the indigo system the redesign plan already proposes (REDESIGN.md §4.1), pairing the primary indigo with a warm amber accent so the brand has both trust and energy — a job board needs both.

Reads at 16 px? Marginal — the half-sun + horizon line both survive; rays disappear. The shape is distinctive enough at small sizes despite the loss of detail. Use case: homepage hero, OG share images, "Welcome" emails, app launch screen. Risk: sunrise marks have been done (Pinterest's red-P-on-white isn't this but the concept space includes Capital One, Sunlife, Sun Microsystems). Differentiation comes from the high-contrast indigo+amber combination.
Recommendation — Concept 01 (Geometric "E" with spark) is the strongest single choice for Employed today:
  1. It scales cleanly from 16 px favicon to 512 px PWA icon without needing a second mark.
  2. It carries letterform recognition (instant "E = Employed") that pure-symbol marks lack on a brand-new platform.
  3. The amber spark dot gives the mark a personality cue that's missing from the current flat red square — and it's literally a single circle, so it's almost free.
  4. It pairs naturally with the wordmark from Concept 04 — same Montserrat 800, same indigo, same amber accent.
If you pick one or two directions, next step is:
  1. Refine the chosen mark (custom paths for the letterform / pin / door / target / sun, tuned at every size, not parametric rects).
  2. Export final standalone SVGs to public/images/ — logo.svg, logo-dark.svg, favicon.svg.
  3. Regenerate the PNG variants (apple-touch-icon.png, favicon-16x16.png, favicon-32x32.png, icon-192x192.png, icon-512x512.png, maskable variants).
  4. Update public/manifest.json theme_color from the current red to #4F46E5.
  5. Wire the new mark into [client/views/includes/header.html](../../client/views/includes/header.html) and [client/views/includes/footer.html](../../client/views/includes/footer.html) (already a lockup; just swap the SVG file).
